# Business Model: Independent Physiotherapy Support Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-3255`
- ISCO-08: `3255`
- Occupation: Physiotherapy Technicians and Assistants
- Social impact: care-access, patient-safety, local-jobs

## Customer

- individual clients
- clinics
- rehabilitation centers

## Offer

- intake and session scheduling
- exercise-equipment support
- session documentation
- escalation pathway
- reporting

## Revenue

- per-session fee
- monthly practice platform fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no direct physical patient manipulation without governor gate
- fall-risk exercises always require human sign-off
- session records are auditable, not editable
